
# Plant based



Higher \[Healthful Plant-Based Diet Index\] hPDI scores were associated with a significantly reduced risk of esophageal and gastric cancers, indicating that adherence to a healthy plant-based diet may reduce upper GI cancer risk. These findings highlight the potential role of dietary modification in upper GI cancer prevention
