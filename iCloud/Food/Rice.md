
Quick simple

- Fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds in a decent amount of oil.
- Fry rice for a minute or so in that oil
- Add 2x ml of water (2x works for 150mg of rice but might be less of a ratio for more rice)
- Bring to boil, then simmer for 10minutes
- Turn off and leave for 10 minutes

Longer may want to boil and get rid of water to remove arsenic like below.

Boil in lots of water for 5min, get rid of water, then cook with the right amount of water for rest of time.

This gets rids of arsenic in white and brown rice.
