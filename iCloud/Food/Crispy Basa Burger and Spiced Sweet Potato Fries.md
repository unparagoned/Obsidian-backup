## Description

Inspired by the South American fried fish sandwiches like po' boys, this burger breads the basa in spicy crumbs, sandwiching it in a bun with mayonnaise, rocket and burger sauce to offset the heat. Serve with sweet potato chips and you've got a hearty taste of the coastal states.

## Instructions

[Download](https://www.hellofresh.co.uk/recipecards/card/698caab28e0d10536157d330-en-GB.pdf)

1

![[37a56d78d6435f0be9d62ddd91dc8ffd_MD5.jpg]]

Spice the Fries

Preheat your oven to 220°C/200°C fan/gas mark 7.

Chop the **sweet potatoes** lengthways into 1cm slices, then chop into 1cm wide fries (no need to peel).

Pop the **fries** onto a large baking tray. Sprinkle over **half** the **Central American style spice mix**. Drizzle with **oil,** season with **salt** and **pepper,**then toss to coat. Spread out in a single layer. **TIP:**_Use two baking trays if necessary_.

When the oven is hot, roast on the top shelf until golden, 25-30 mins. Turn halfway through.

2

![[ef5c7a9a87ba945562dd21c17f58f42a_MD5.jpg]]

Make the Dressing

Meanwhile, quarter, core and thinly slice the **apple** (no need to peel).

In a large bowl, combine the **red wine vinegar**with the **sugar** and **olive oil for the dressing** (see pantry for both amounts). Set aside. 

3

![[714a682cd286a60bdae5b6624cbf552c_MD5.jpg]]

Bread the Basa

Slice the **basa fillets** in half widthways. 

Crack the **egg** (see pantry for amount) into a medium bowl and whisk.

Put the **breadcrumbs** and remaining **Central American style spice mix** into another bowl, season with the **salt** (see pantry for amount) and **pepper.**

Season the **fish,** then dip the **fish** into the **egg**and then the **breadcrumbs,** ensuring it's completely coated. Transfer to a clean plate. **IMPORTANT:**_Wash your hands and equipment after handling raw fish and discard any excess egg_.

4

![[b94b56ef8e1ff693b239c84653180533_MD5.jpg]]

Fry the Basa

Once the **fish** is coated, pop a large frying pan on high heat and add enough **oil** to coat the bottom of the pan. **TIP:**_You want the oil to be hot so the fish fries properly - heat for 2-3 mins before you add the fish_.

When hot, lay the **fish** in the pan and cook until crispy and golden, 6-8 mins. Turn every couple of mins.

Once cooked, transfer to a plate lined with kitchen paper. **IMPORTANT:**_The fish is cooked when opaque in the middle_.

5

![[9f8b01a0a79fd94d8aa6a7f2e878e0bd_MD5.jpg]]

Warm the Buns

Next, halve the **burger buns.** Pop into the oven to warm through, 2-3 mins.

When everything's ready, add the **rocket** and **apple slices** to the bowl with the **dressing.** Toss to evenly coat the **rocket leaves.** 

6

![[3fd8cd33716ce184b99af97ceadedac4_MD5.jpg]]

Serve

Spread the **burger sauce** over the **bun lids** and **half** the **mayonnaise** (see pantry for amount) over the **bun bases.**

Top the **bases** with the **fish burgers** and some of the **rocket leaves,** then sandwich shut with the **bun lids.**

Serve with the **sweet potato chips** and remaining **salad** on the side.

Add the remaining **mayonnaise** on the side of your plates for dipping the **chips.**
