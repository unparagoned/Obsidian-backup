
# Type

Yellowfin is medium in terms of mercury and has good taste.

# Ingredients

- Chilli pepper
- Sesame seed oil
- 0.5 bottle Sweet chilli sauce.
- Ginger
- Chinese 5 spice
- 2 limes/lemons
- Tuna steak

# Cooking

- Chop up and mix all ingredients.
- Get pan very hot - 14 was too hot
- Cook no more than 30/45s on each side.

# Sides

- Frozen veg
- Jacket Potato
