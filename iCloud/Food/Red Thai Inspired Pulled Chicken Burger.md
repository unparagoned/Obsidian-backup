## Instructions

1

![Get Started](https://media.hellofresh.com/w_750,q_auto,f_auto,c_limit,fl_lossy/hellofresh_s3/697a56fc9629deb2e47d7d30/step-b143bb00.jpg)

Get Started

Preheat your oven to 220°C/200°C fan/gas mark 7.

Chop the **potatoes** lengthways into 1cm slices, then chop into 1cm wide chips (no need to peel). 

Pop the **chips** onto a large baking tray. Drizzle with **oil**, season with **salt** and **pepper**, then toss to coat. Spread out in a single layer. **TIP:**_Use two baking trays if necessary._ 

When the oven is hot, bake on the top shelf until golden, 25-30 mins.Turn halfway through.

2

![Roast your Chicken](https://media.hellofresh.com/w_750,q_auto,f_auto,c_limit,fl_lossy/hellofresh_s3/697a56fc9629deb2e47d7d30/step-a723e510.jpg)

Roast your Chicken

In the meantime, remove the **chicken** from the packaging and place in an ovenproof dish, discarding the juices.

Season with **salt** and **pepper** and spread over the**red Thai style paste**.

Roast on the middle shelf of your oven for 20-25 mins.**IMPORTANT:**_Ensure the chicken is piping hot throughout._

3

![Finish the Prep](https://media.hellofresh.com/w_750,q_auto,f_auto,c_limit,fl_lossy/hellofresh_s3/697a56fc9629deb2e47d7d30/step-4bb9cc20.jpg)

Finish the Prep

In the meantime, zest and halve the **lime**. 

Halve the **burger buns** and set aside for later. 

4

![Coleslaw Time](https://media.hellofresh.com/w_750,q_auto,f_auto,c_limit,fl_lossy/hellofresh_s3/697a56fc9629deb2e47d7d30/step-bc0e8630.jpg)

Coleslaw Time 

In a large bowl, combine the **mayonaise**,**half** the **sweet chilli sauce** and **half** the **lime juice**.

Toss the **rocket** and **coleslaw mix** through the **mayo dressing**. Season with **salt** and **pepper**, then set aside for later.

When the **chips** are at least halfway through cooking, toss through **lime zest** and return to the oven for the remaining cooking time.

5

![Finishing Touches](https://media.hellofresh.com/w_750,q_auto,f_auto,c_limit,fl_lossy/hellofresh_s3/697a56fc9629deb2e47d7d30/step-b54dfb40.jpg)

Finishing Touches

Once the **chicken** is cooked, remove from the oven. Pop the **buns** onto a baking tray and into the oven to warm through, 2-3 mins.

Meanwhile, use two forks to gently break apart the **chicken**. 

Mix the remaining**sweet chilli sauce** and **lime juice** through the **pulled chicken**. Taste and season with **salt** and **pepper** if needed. 

6

![Finish and Serve](https://media.hellofresh.com/w_750,q_auto,f_auto,c_limit,fl_lossy/hellofresh_s3/697a56fc9629deb2e47d7d30/step-29364550.jpg)

Finish and Serve

When everything's ready, transfer the **buns** to your serving plates. Add a handful of the**slaw mixture** and top with your **Thai style spiced pulled chicken**.

Sandwich shut with the **bun lids**.

Serve your **zesty chips** and remaining **slaw**alongside.
