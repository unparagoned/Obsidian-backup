

## Cozy Lentils I've Made A Thousand Times

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a3wKB9coGwU
1 Medium onion, diced 3 Bell pepper, diced small 2 Medium potatoes, diced small Optional: Celery or carrot (if you want a classic mirepoix) SEASONINGS: 1 Tbsp coriander seeds, ground 1 Tbsp fennel seeds, ground ¾ Tsp anise seeds, ground 1 Tsp white peppercorns, ground 2 Bay leaves 6 Cloves garlic, sliced 1 Lemon, zest and juice Pinch dried chili flakes, ground Stew Base: 3 Tbsp tomato paste 1 Cup French green lentils (rinsed well ) 1 Can crushed tomatoes (no salt added) 4 Cups water ( add extra if needed) 2 Bay leaves 1 Tbsp Vegetable bouillon paste (instead of salt) Salt and black pepper to taste 1 Tsp smoked paprika 1 Handful Fresh parsley, chopped Salt and black pepper to taste Instructions 1. Prep the vegetables Dice onions, peppers, and potatoes 2. Make the spice-garlic-lemon paste In a mortar and pestle, crush coriander, fennel, anise, white peppercorns, and chili flakes until coarse. Add the sliced garlic and lemon zest. Pound again into a fragrant paste. 3. Fry the tomato paste Heat olive oil over medium-high and add 3 tbsp tomato paste. Cook until it starts to darken and caramelizes. Add the spice paste and fry briefly to bloom the aromatics. 4. Add the vegetables Add all diced vegetables to the pot. Stir to coat in the tomato-spice mixture and cook until they soften. 5. Add lentils and liquids Stir in rinsed French green lentils. Add crushed tomatoes, 4 cups water, lemon juice, and bay leaves. 6. Simmer Once it comes to slow boil, add potatoes. Lower heat to medium-low and simmer until lentils and potatoes are tender. Add more water if it thickens too quickly. 7. Season and finish Taste. Add veggie Boullion . Add smoked paprika, adjust salt and pepper, and finish with fresh parsley.
