

# I Tested 4 Salmon Types, Only One Wins
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mZTvUsHzf2o

- Oil and salt all over
- score three lines down the skin side
- Cook in a medium low heat around 3 min till it's at a third of the way through.
- Flip, turn heat to low, add butter and baste it
- Scottish and Atlantic are good. King is good as well but very soft.
