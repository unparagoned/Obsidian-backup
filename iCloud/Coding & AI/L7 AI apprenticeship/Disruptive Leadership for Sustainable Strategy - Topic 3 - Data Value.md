## The big data business model maturity index

Data driven isn't enough, you need to be value driven

Exploit data to drive new sources of customers, product and operational value.


Big data business model maturity index

![[Pasted image 20260127140625.png]]


To move from business monitoring to business insights, use predictive analytics to make actionable insights. 

To move from business insights to business optimisation, embeds analytics into SOP. Supported by suitable technology like data lakes. 

Business optimisation to insight monetisation, aim of developing new products, services, channels, partners, audiences and markets.

Insight monetisation to digital transformation. Is more about culture than data an anlytics.

## Data science value engineering framework

![[Pasted image 20260127142938.png]]

### Business initiative

Can be selected from current strategic plan.

- Be critical to immediate term business success
- Documented
- Cross-functional
- Be owned or championed by a senior business executive
- Has actionable and measurable financial goal
- Has a delivery framework between 12 and 8 months

Once decided set metrics or KPIs

### Identify key business steak-holders

A persona should be created for each stakeholder.

### Brainstorm and prioritise decisions(use cases)

Do value calculation and estimated cost of implementation.
![[Pasted image 20260127143413.png]]

### Identify supporting analytics

Identify a set of questions for the use cases. Such that the answers would create value. 
The stakeholders would be asked what they would do if the answer was x, y, z.
