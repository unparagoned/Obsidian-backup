Potential Questions for the Presentation

  

Tips: map the following questions to your project, answer all of them, have the sheet with the questions and answers ready in case you need it during the presentation.

  

- How did you arrive to use this x model? Why not y model?

- What if you model goes terrible wrong one day? How will you fix it?

- What is the bias in your project? How to mitigate?

- What is ROI of your project?

- How will you handle the project scope extension in the future?

- How does your project impact people day to day life & work?

- How to do communicate with higher management level?

- Will you present the same content if you are asked to present to external audience? Like customer or company competitor?

- How this project initiate? Where is this project coming from?

- What is your data pipeline like?

- Where is data coming from? same resource or different resources?

- What trade-offs did you make?

- What is the lesson learned in this project?

- What is your current granularity in your forecasting project? How will you handle if the stakeholder wants to see more granular results?

  

You may get some science questions related to the models you have used in your project.

  

e.g.

  

- What metrics are used to evaluate regression models?

- What metrics are used to evaluate classification models?

- What was the limitation of LSTM and the basis for emergence of transformer models? (if your project is in language generation area)
