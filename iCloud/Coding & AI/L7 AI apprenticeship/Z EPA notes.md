
# Project report - report and presentation - graded as one.

Once through gateway - 6 weeks timeline.

Online presentation - independent assessor.

Presentation 30min

Q & A 45 min

Review draft

# Professional discussion

Can have journal with you. Can refer to this as much as you want.

60min

10 open questions

4 long response questions. Wholistically marked.

Online

100 min
