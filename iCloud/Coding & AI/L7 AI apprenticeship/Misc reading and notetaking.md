SQ4R is a reading and notetaking technique

Survey

Question - turn headings and subheadings into questions

Read - one section at a time in detail, look for answers to question and add any new questions

Respond - answers to questions, paraphrased in own words

Reflect - make links to what you've previously read, organisational context

Review
