**Foreword**

  

This Assessment Method investigates a set of competencies from the following themes:

  

• Use and knowledge of computing and statistical foundations of AI and data science

  

• Professional practice in a commercial environment

  

• Awareness of the current and future impact of AI and data science on industry and society

  

• Development of suitable AI and data science solutions, with consideration for ethical, legal, regulatory and governance issues

  

• Continuous Professional Development

  

The method is not underpinned or supported by a summative portfolio of evidence. However, students can have a journal with them to help them remember what they have learnt and done in order to answer questions. The journal is not submitted to the examiner.

  

You have likely been producing a journal as you have moved through the course. Your journal will have been organised in terms of standards. For the professional discussion, the examiners group the assessment method standards into pass criteria so I think you might be better off organising the notes that you can take with you into the professional discussion according to the same pass criteria. This template is organised accordingly. You are not obliged to use it, of course, and are free to prepare the journal you take into the professional discussion in any way you wish.

  

Sometimes, the examiners go quite hard into the maths and algorithms behind the methods you have been using so be sure to revise and make notes on those. Look at the companion document "Preparing for the Profession Discussion" , particularly the appendix, which provides the likely questions.

  

The idea of this document is just to give you a template. You have to complete it with relevant notes. You can probably transfer relevant parts of your standard journal here , plus make new notes particularly of relevant maths and algorithms.

  

Best of luck. Having reached this stage of the programme, I have every confidence you can succeed.

  

Anne James

  

November 2024

Regression

  

Decision Trees

**Area 2: Professional practice in a commercial environment** (K8, K10, S6, S8, S14, S23, S28, B1, B4, B7)

  

Make notes on:

  

- The different types of machine learning

- The machine learning pipeline

- Train- validate - test

  

Also make notes to be able to answer other questions that might come up as specified in the "Preparing for the Professional Discussion " document. Include evidence of your experience where applicable.

  

How do you select and apply the most effective/appropriate AI and data science techniques to solve complex business problems?

  

How do you disseminate AI and data science practices?

**Area 4:** **Development of suitable AI and data science solutions, with consideration for ethical, legal, regulatory and governance issues** (K29, S12, B3)

  

For this section you need to show knowledge of **GPDR** and other relevant codes .

  

See "Preparing for the Professional Discussion" document appendix for types of question that might come up here and make notes including evidence.

  

As well as GDPR, you need to be able to discuss more general **ethics** with regard to **implications of AI to society**

  

Also in this section prepare some notes on **accessibility**.

  

How do you make sure the systems you develop are accessible? How can AI help with accessibility?

  

What procedures does your own organisation have with regard to accessibility.

  

**Area 5: Continuous Professional Development** (B5, B8)

  

Make a list of CPD courses you have done while on programme. These can include any QA has specified as well as any undertaken independently.

  

If you are a team leader, consider also how you can help your team to keep up with developments.

  

Note also any professional community engagement you have.

  

Reading books and journals is also a suitable answer here but you may be asked what you have read so list some here together with brief summary or your reaction to them
