## Overview of the Disruptive Leadership for Sustainable Strategy Practice Technical Test

Advances in automation, data availability, and artificial intelligence are increasingly shaping how organisations address complex business challenges. As organisations adopt digital and AI enabled solutions, there is a growing need for professionals who can design, justify, and evaluate technically sound data driven solutions that align with organisational objectives and constraints.

This practice technical test is designed to prepare you for the End Point Assessment technical test by simulating the type of scenario and questioning you will encounter. You will be presented with a fictional organisational scenario that requires you to propose an AI focused solution to a defined problem. Your responses should demonstrate your ability to apply data science and AI principles in a structured, practical, and business focused way.

Using the knowledge and skills developed throughout this module, you are expected to show how you would identify appropriate data sources, manage and analyse data, select and justify suitable machine learning approaches, and consider legal, ethical, and organisational factors that may affect implementation. The emphasis of this assessment is on technical reasoning, decision making, and your ability to explain how an AI solution could be developed and deployed within a real-world organisational context.

This activity mirrors the structure and expectations of the EPA technical test. While the scenario in the real assessment will be different, the nature of the questions and the competencies being assessed will be the same or very similar. The practice test therefore provides an opportunity to rehearse your approach, structure your answers effectively, and ensure you can clearly evidence the relevant knowledge, skills, and behaviours under test conditions.

**The assignment consists of one task:**

- A practice technical test centred around an AI-focussed solution development in an organisation.
