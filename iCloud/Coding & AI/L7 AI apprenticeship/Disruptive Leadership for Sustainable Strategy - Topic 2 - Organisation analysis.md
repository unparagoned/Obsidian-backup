Score 0-5

0. Unaware. Over use of spreadsheets, data is everywhere and anyone can modify it
1. Aware. Few processes beyond what is required by regulation.
2. Reactive. Emerging processes not monitored for data quality, respond to issue of data breach
3. Proactive. Process developed by information stewards with little authority. Master data management and governance of unstructured data at infancy.
4. Managed. Governance in place with enterprise-level stewards and champions
5. Optimised. All information assets are inventoried, true partnerships with customers and suppliers are established, and data landscape is trusted and agile
