The following items need to be recorded in your PDP: 

- Career goals. 
- Objectives (stepping stones) needed to meet the goals. 
- Activities identified to help you reach the objectives. 
- Time by which each activity should be complete. 
- Measure of whether the activity has been completed or not over time.
