DD Born again S2E08 finalle

I don't think it's uncontroversial that a show needs a good storyline and for it to make sense. I personally think it should be clever as well. For a finale it should bring together various elements.

First it's all based around that trial. In previous episodes it was established that they weren't completely out of touch of reality and were also biased towards Fisk. So based on how they setup trials to work, what happened in the last episode, didn't make any sense, let alone have any chance of working.

There is this idea that you can use luck/writing to get people into trouble, but you shouldn't use luck/writing to get people out of trouble. But this was a finale, which kind of means it's the worse place to use luck/writing to resolve things.

No-one would ever care about DD testimony or believed it was reliable, plus he didn't even technically give that testimony. No Judges would have dropped it all based on what happened. It's the kind of logic that would work in a 90's show, but not anymore.

So everything to get Karen off, was just stupid, it doesn't make sense in the real world, and more importantly it doesn't even make sense in the DD world setup.

The way Fisk acted and talked in that last episode didn't really make sense. In the rest of the show he was mainly using his position. He didn't really care about it. But here that's flipped around.

There was no clever or satisfying story or ending. It was simply Fisk loses.

Like I can't even see how people would have liked it, if you can say why you thought it was one of the best endings I can focus on those points.
