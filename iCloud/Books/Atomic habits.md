
Point and talk loudly about habits good or bad. Eg I don’t need this cookie, it’ll make me fat

Making a plan is key, that way you just follow the plan rather than needing to make the decision

Habit stacking. After x I will y

After I eat dinner, I will clean up kitchen 

I will x at time in location

Set up environment to make things easier

Set certain places or devices for different uses. Eg breakfast bar for eating, soda for TV, desk for work

Reframe things so think about habits you want to do positively and negative habits negatively. 

Link three breaths and smiling with something that makes you happy. Then can do the breaths other times to make you happy. 


Prime the environment to make it easy. Eg put out gym clothes three night before

Increase friction for bad habits

2minute rule, just have a very easy low friction habit like read for two minutes. It so naturally increases but by keeping it simple means you’ll do it. You want a gateway habit eg putting on your running shoes


When saving money transfer it to an account with a name on it. This proves you with an instant reward

Tracking habits it’s important. Combine it with habit stacking,?eg after taking vitamins record it

Never miss twice. 

Don’t make perfection the goal, if you miss one habit don’t make it an excuse not to do others

It’s better to just go to the gym for 5 min, then completely miss it

It’s not about what happens on a workout, is about the habit
