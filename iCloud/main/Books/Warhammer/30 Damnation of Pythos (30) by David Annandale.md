9/10 Good book and great ending
 
Some iron hands safe some raven guard and salamaders at isstavan V. They find Pythos, where an artifact lets a psycher connect and see more stuff from the warp. They use the info to ambush the enemy.
 
They return the Pythos, and there are loads of humans in ships landing. The animals there start killing the humans, but the space marines save some, set up a base for them. Then there is an artifact at their base, they use the ship to destroy it, but it destroys the ship.
 
They go down a shaft and see a portal for demons. The demons and animals fight. The space marines kill the humans, but it's a safcrifice bringing a demon.
 
They take the astropath to the artifact, so she sends a warning to earth. But it was short and probably just said demons, and ignored.
