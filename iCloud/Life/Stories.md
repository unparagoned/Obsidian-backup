## Cloud

This one time I was skydiving and we separated(broke off) lower than we were supposed to and on top of that there was cloud right below me(you aren't supposed to go through clouds so it's not a regular thing). As I was flying away from everyone else(tracking) I saw the silhouette of someone right were I was going. I tried to avoid them but there wasn't time, I just accepted my fate, then just before I hit the them I closed my eyes, no bang like I expected. A second later I realised I was still alive and it hit me that it wasn't the silhouette of someone else but my shadow.
