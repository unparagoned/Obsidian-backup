

A claim would be that "Jesus rose from the grave", testimony would be "I saw Jesus rose from the grave". So the claims is different from your testimonial statement. So the claim "Jesus rose from the grave" is not evidence that "Jesus rose from the grave". Testimonial that someone saw Jesus rose from the grave is not a claim but it is testimonial evidence even if it's low quality.

Is "I saw Jesus rise from the grave" not itself a claim? Aren't all testimonies also claims?

I would say "I saw Jesus rise from the grave" has a claim that is what they experienced. But people wouldn't use it just for that end, they would want to use that as evidence for the claim that "Jesus rose from the grave". So their claim is evidence in respect to the main claim they want to prove. So they are still kind of distinct even if they are linked.
